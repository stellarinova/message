Divinity & Pre-Existence

The Eternal "I AM"

"Most assuredly, I say to you, before Abraham was, I AM." John 8:58

Insight: A direct claim to divinity. Jesus asserts He is eternal, using the name God used for Himself in Exodus.

Oneness with the Father

"I and My Father are one." John 10:30

Insight: Jesus declares unity of essence with God. Distinct in person, but identical in nature.

Identity & Mission

The Messiah

"I am. And you will see the Son of Man sitting at the right hand of the Power..." Mark 14:62
Key Message: Confirmation that He is the promised Savior and the Cosmic Judge.

Prophecy Fulfilled

"The Spirit of the Lord is upon Me... Today this Scripture is fulfilled in your hearing." Luke 4:18-21
Key Message: He is the Anointed One sent to liberate the oppressed and proclaim God's favor.

Seeker of the Lost

"For the Son of Man has come to seek and to save that which was lost." Luke 19:10
Key Message: His primary mission is to actively pursue and rescue those separated from God.
